Case Studies

Establishing a Center of Excellence (CoE):

Worked with a Fortune 500 industrial company generating ~$200M in annual savings. Work included: designing CoE architecture and organization, creating global PCBA sourcing strategy (including supplier selection, evaluation process, quoting and negotiation process.

Evolving Medical Device companies supply chain:

Generating a more flexible and dynamic organization, delivering ~$100M in savings. Work included: developing sourcing strategy, creating capability training protocol and coaching client through high impact negotiations.

Changing the way sourcing and design work together:

Establishing a new organization while delivering ~$30M+ in annual savings. Work included: Re-imaging the marketing/design/sourcing relationship. Enabling more actionable conversations around what customers need and how to effectively collaborate cross functionally to achieve these goals.

Establishing excellence in negotiations, leading:

We have led in both the Pharma and Consumer Health industries. Work included: Leading the efforts to build capabilities and coach client team through difficult sole sourced CMO negotiations. Pharma client $115M, Consumer Health $20M+ annual run rate savings.

Revising the multi-year commodity strategy of a large aerospace client:

This ended up resulting in ~$65M+ of annual run rate savings.  Work included: Developing strategies for 15+ commodities, quantifying the opportunity available, and creating the supplier playbook to execute.  Led to co-leading the effort to re-organize the sourcing and procurement team and add capabilities to execute on our efforts.

Revised the negotiation and sourcing capabilities of a top Medical Device company, resulting in $100M+ in annual run rate savings:

Working with the Division COO and VP of Sourcing developed a strategy to identify direct materials deflation synergies the result of a large acquisition, introducing the processes and capabilities needed to sustain the “wins” long term. Designed, presented the proposal to the client leadership and executed the effort.
