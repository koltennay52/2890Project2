Bennett Gutmann

Founder and Managing Director

Bennett Gutmann spent 20 years in the manufacturing industry specializing in operations working for notable companies such as Celestica, Flextronics, and Solectron. In the past 7 years, he worked with Mckinsey and Company as a consultant before starting Attigo Inc. Bennett’s expertise is in supplier negotiations, outsource manufacturing, procurement, supply chain management and operational management in the medical device industry, aerospace, and industrial equipment. He has a successful track record of increasing revenue and growing profit, while driving continuous improvement initiatives to increase productivity and reduce costs for his Clients. He is a dedicated, caring leader skilled at enabling organizational change and developing leaders capable of leading the change effort on their own. When not working, Bennett likes to spend time with his wife and two kids.

JoAnna Le

Managing Partner, Head of Marketing

JoAnna spent over 17 years in Corporate Banking for various large Banks such as Bank of the West, Union Bank, and BBVA. She helped large corporate clients achieve their goals through lending and banking needs. Prior to joining Attigo, she worked at a food start up helping them with their marketing and finance strategies.
